Display the lines of all users who have at least 5 vowels in their username.
grep -i -E "^[^:]*([aeiou][^:]*){5,}:" /etc/passwd

-i = ignore upper case/ lower case
-v = not

Display the lines of all the users not having bash as their shell.
grep -v "/bash$" /etc/passwd

Display the lines of all users named Ion. We will have to search in the user info field 
(the fifth field) of each line.
grep -i -E "^([^:]*:){4}[^:]*\<ion\>" /etc/passwd

Display all lines containing an email address
What does an email address look like?
It has 1. username
	2. @
	3. hostname (yahoo.com or yahoo.co.uk)

grep -i -E "\<[^@*\!?]+@[a-z0-9_-]+(.[a-z0-9_-]+)+\>" a.txt
